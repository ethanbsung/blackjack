1. Shuffle Deck
2. Give Player 1 card face up
3. Give Dealer 1 card face down
4. Give Player 2nd card face up
5. Give Dealer 2nd card face up
6. Player has option to hit, stand, double, or split
7. Depending on what player does, dealer will give player more cards, or flip over their card
8. If player goes over 21, they bust and dealer wins
9. Else, dealer shows both cards
10. If dealer has less than 17, they deal themself cards until they get >= 17
11. If dealer gets > 21, they bust and player wins
